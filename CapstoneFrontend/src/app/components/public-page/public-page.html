What We Cover

Comprehensive protection for every scenario

Accidental Death

Full coverage amount paid to your nominated beneficiary in case of accidental death.

Permanent Total Disability

A percentage of coverage for permanent total disability due to an accident.

Permanent Partial Disability

Partial coverage for permanent partial disabilities resulting from accidents.

Temporary Total Disability

Weekly compensation during your recovery period from temporary disability.

Filing a Claim

Quick and transparent claims process

Submit Claim

Select your policy, describe the incident, and attach supporting documents.

Under Review

A dedicated Claims Officer reviews and assesses your claim.

Approved / Rejected

You are notified of the decision along with the approved amount.

Settlement

Approved claims are settled and the amount is disbursed to you.
